Background

Background info, School and Home.

My name is Nathan Perrine, I'm 32 years old, live with my long term girlfriend and two cats, and I have a mild gaming and Diet Coke addiction. I've lived in Ohio all my life, but I've traveled to Canada and Mexico, and various states for vacations. I went to college after High School and while I did well enough, a lack of dedication and mental health issues kept me from completing my degree. Over the years I tried to teach myself programming from various online sources and while I learned a good deal of information I didn't have the time to properly apply it and move to the next level.

Just around Christmas last year the company I had worked at the last six years was bought out. All existing employees were told our wages were being reduced to their company sandards (just above minimum wage) and we no longer qualified for vacation or benefits for another year, including the vacation I was scheduled to leave on in two weeks that was approved for the last nine months. My boss, two coworkers and myself decided together it wasn't worth it and we all quit. And that brought me here, with the free time after vacation I was trying to decide how to move forward. I could go to any store or restaurant near me and get a job but I wanted to find something better that can properly support my family. After doing a lot of reading I had decided the best way forward for me would be a bootcamp.
